Must-Have Shooting Accessories

AccessoryDescription
PowderBlack powder or substitute used as a propellant in muzzleloading firearms.
FlintNatural or synthetic flint used in flintlock firearms to create sparks and ignite powder.
CapsPercussion caps used to ignite the powder charge in percussion firearms.
BallsLead or other materials shaped into projectiles for muzzleloading firearms.
Casting LeadLead ingots used for bullet casting, providing customizable ammunition options.
PatchesThin fabric or material used as a cushion between the ball and the barrel, ensuring a proper seal.
LubeLubricant applied to the patch to reduce friction and aid in smooth barrel loading.
Shooting BagsSturdy bags designed to carry and organize muzzleloading accessories in the field.
Powder HornsDecorative containers for storing and dispensing black powder during shooting sessions.
CleaningTools and supplies for maintaining and cleaning the barrel and other firearm components.
