Jonathan Taylor Thomas And Scarlett Pomers Aren't The Only Child Stars To Keep Their Lives A Secret

Publish date: 2024-08-02

Jonathan Taylor Thomas and Scarlett Pomers are just two examples of child stars who rose to fame at a young age, only to disappear from the public eye and keep their lives private. Thomas was a popular child actor in the 90s, best known for his role on the sitcom Home Improvement, while Pomers starred in the series Reba in the early 2000s. Both actors left Hollywood at a young age and have since kept a low profile, leaving fans wondering what happened to them.

However, they are not the only child stars to vanish from Hollywood and keep their lives a secret. Over the years, many child actors have experienced fame and success at a young age, only to step away from the spotlight and lead private lives. These former child stars have left lasting impressions on Hollywood and their fans, despite their decision to live more private lives.

8 What Happened To Jonathan Taylor Thomas?

According to We Got This Covered, Jonathan Taylor Thomas, who rose to fame as a child star playing Randy Taylor in the hit TV show Home Improvement, took a break from acting in the early 2000s to focus on his education.

After graduating from Columbia University in 2010, he made a brief return to acting with guest appearances on shows like Veronica Mars and Last Man Standing. However, since then, he has largely stayed out of the public eye and kept his personal life private.

In a rare interview with People in 2013, Thomas explained that he enjoys working behind the scenes in the entertainment industry and is still involved in show business in that capacity. He also revealed that he values his privacy and prefers to keep his personal life out of the spotlight in fact, he does not currently have an Instagram account.

7 What Happened To Scarlett Pomers?

Scarlett Pomers, who is best known for her role as Kyra Hart on the hit TV show Reba, has chosen to delete her social media accounts and is not currently active on any social media platform.

According to Looper, after taking a break from acting in the mid-2000s to focus on her music career, she also struggled with an eating disorder, which she has since spoken about openly.

Pomers has become an advocate for eating disorder awareness and has shared her story to help others who may be going through similar struggles. In addition to her advocacy work for eating disorder awareness, Pomers has also pursued her passion for photography and has released several music albums over the years.

6 Amanda Bynes Left Hollywood In 2010

Bynes was a popular child star in the late 90s and early 2000s, starring in shows such as All That and The Amanda Show, as well as films like She's the Man and Hairspray. In the mid-2010s, she experienced a public breakdown and took a break from acting.

While Bynes has been keeping a low profile in terms of her public appearances and acting career, she has been open about her journey toward healing and self-improvement.

5 Did Taran Noah Smith Also Step Away From Hollywood?

Taran Noah Smith is best known for playing the role of Mark Taylor on the hit TV series Home Improvement. After the show ended in 1999, Smith decided to step away from acting and focus on his personal interests. He has since become an advocate for sustainable living and has been involved in organic farming and eco-friendly construction.

In addition to his work in sustainable living and activism, Taran Noah Smith has also pursued entrepreneurship. He opened a food company called Playfood, which focused on organic and locally sourced ingredients. The company aimed to promote healthy eating habits and to provide affordable, sustainable food options to the community.

4 Where Is Alex D. Linz Now?

Alex D. Linz, who starred in popular films such as Home Alone 3 and Max Keeble's Big Move, made a decision to step away from the entertainment industry after completing his college education.

Since then, he has largely kept his personal life out of the public eye. After graduating from high school, Linz continued his education and earned a Bachelor of Arts degree in Science, Technology, and Society from the prestigious University of California. He then reportedly secured employment at a Los Angeles-based firm, where he works as a legal researcher. In addition to his legal work, Linz is also a lead science instructor.

2 Where Is Mara Wilson Now?

Mara Wilson is best known for her roles in popular movies such as Mrs. Doubtfire, Matilda, and Miracle on 34th Street, but she made the bold decision to quit the entertainment industry at just 13 years old. The intense pressures and expectations of the industry were too much for her, and she decided to pursue a life on her own terms.

Since leaving Hollywood, Wilson has focused on her education and writing. She earned a degree in playwriting from New York University and has since published a memoir titled Where Am I Now? True Stories of Girlhood and Accidental Fame. In addition to her writing, Wilson is an advocate for mental health awareness and has been open about her own struggles with anxiety and depression.

1 Where Is Alisan Porter Today?

According to Insider, Alisan Porter, who rose to fame as the adorable child star in the movie Curly Sue, chose a different path after leaving the entertainment industry. Porter decided to pursue a career in music and has since become a successful singer and songwriter.

In 2016, she competed on the tenth season of the singing competition show The Voice and was crowned the winner. She has since released several albums and continues to perform. In addition to her music career, Porter is a voice coach and also a successful blogger. She writes about her experiences with motherhood, addiction recovery, and mental health on her blog, The Lil' Mamas. She runs the blog with her partner Celia Behar.
