Who Is The Richest 'House Bunny' Cast Member In 2022?

Publish date: 2024-07-13

The 2008 comedy movie The House Bunny follows the story of a former Playboy bunny as she becomes the house mother of an unpopular university sorority. The movie grossed $70.4 million and since its release, it has gained a cult following and is known as one of the best college movies.

Kicking the list off is Kiely Williams who plays Lilly Marsen in The House Bunny.
