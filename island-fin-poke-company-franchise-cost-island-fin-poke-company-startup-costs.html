Island Fin Poke Franchise Training Program

At Island Fin Poke Company, we understand the importance of providing our franchisees with the tools and knowledge they need to succeed. That’s why we offer a comprehensive training program that equips each franchisee with the skills necessary to operate their own successful poke restaurant.

Our training program consists of:

  • 17 hours of classroom training
  • 23 hours of on-the-job training
  • 8 days of training at the franchisee’s location

During the classroom training, franchisees will learn about our brand, our menu items, and our operational procedures. They will also receive training on customer service, sales techniques, and marketing strategies.

The on-the-job training provides hands-on experience in a real-world poke restaurant setting. Franchisees will learn every aspect of the day-to-day operations, including food preparation, inventory management, and staff management.

Finally, the in-location training allows franchisees to apply what they have learned in their own restaurant. Our experienced trainers will be on-site to provide guidance and support, ensuring a smooth transition to independently operating the franchise.

Island Fin Poke Franchise Visa Candidacy

Island Fin Poke Company is committed to creating opportunities for individuals from abroad who are interested in starting their own franchise in the United States. As part of this commitment, the franchise works closely with E2 visa and EB-5 visa candidates to facilitate their journey towards owning and operating an Island Fin Poke Co. franchise.

The E2 visa is a nonimmigrant visa that allows individuals from treaty countries to live and work in the United States based on a substantial investment in a U.S. business. Island Fin Poke Company provides the necessary support and resources to help E2 visa candidates navigate the visa process and ensure a smooth transition into the franchise business.

The EB-5 visa program offers a path to permanent residency for foreign investors who make a substantial investment in a U.S. business. Island Fin Poke Company collaborates with EB-5 visa candidates, providing the guidance and assistance required to meet the program’s criteria and successfully establish an Island Fin Poke Co. franchise in the United States.
