Here's What Anna Faris Has Really Been Up To Since Her Split From Chris Pratt

Publish date: 2024-07-19

Actress Anna Faris rose to fame in the early 2000s thanks to her portrayal of Cindy Campbell in the Scary Movie franchise. Since then, she established herself as a successful actress known for her iconic rom-coms. In early 2007 Faris married actor Chis Pratt and in 2012 their son was born. Unfortunately, the couple announced their separation in August 2017.

Today, we're taking a look at everything the actress — who is worth $30 million — has been up to since the split. From venturing out into different entertainment businesses to falling n love again — keep on scrolling to see just what Anna Faris has achieved since 2017!

Is Anna Faris Still Acting Or Is She Focused On Her Book And Podcast?

In October 2017 — only two months after the announcement of her separation from Chris Pratt, Faris released her first book. The title of her memoir is Unqualified and in it, the actress shares stories from different stages of her life. The book was very successful and that fall, it even ended up on Amazon's list of top 20 blockbuster books alongside the work of authors such as Dan Brown and Stephen King.

Those who have been keeping up with the actress surely know that just like many other stars in Hollywood, Faris also had a podcast. The podcast, titled Unqualified, launched back in 2015, and since her split from Pratt, Faris managed to grow it even more. On the podcast, the Hollywood star interviews fellow celebs, and so far some of her most prominent guests include Matthew McConaughey, Cameron Diaz, Gwyneth Paltrow, Paris Hilton, and many more. Here's what Faris revealed about how that podcast came to be:

What Happened To Anna Faris' Career?

In 2018 Anna Faris had her first leading role since the 2011 rom-com What's Your Number. Faris played Kate Sullivan in Overboard, a remake of the 1987 movie of the same name which stars Goldie Hawn and Kurt Russell. Apart from Faris, the 2018 rom-com also stars Eugenio Derbez, Eva Longoria, and John Hannah. Overboard tells the story of a single mother who manages to convince a rich gut with amnesia that they are married — and it currently holds a 6.0 rating on IMDb.

Anna Faris is certainly no stranger to voice acting — with projects like Cloudy with a Chance of Meatballs and the Alvin and the Chipmunks movies that star had plenty of success in that field. In 2017 fans could her the actress as Jailbreak in the computer-animated sci-fi comedy The Emoji Movie. Apart from Faris, the rest of the voice cast consists of T.J. Miller, James Corden, Maya Rudolph, Jennifer Coolidge, Christina Aguilera and Sofía Vergara. Currently, the movie — which is based on emojis — has a 3.3 rating on IMDb.

Is Anna Faris Dating Anyone In 2022?

A month after announcing her separation from Chris Pratt, the actress started dating cinematographer Michael Barrett. Last year, Faris accidentally revealed that the two got married by saying this:

"My husband shot a movie in Japan, and he speaks about this town that specialized in soba noodles. He would love to live in Japan for a year. My dream would be Venice."

How Does Anna Faris Feel About Her Divorce From Chris Pratt?

While Faris and Pratt separated in peace, the actress did eventually reveal more details about their relationship and divorce. Here's what the actress said about her experience:

"For me, I think after every breakup, at some point I realize that there were a lot of things I ignored that I really shouldn't have. In hindsight, it felt like my hand was forced. I don't think it was ever an independent decision. I think it stunted me in a lot of ways. One of them being that I never talked about any issues, so to the people, even who I was closest to, I'm sure things were more transparent with my relationship with Ben, but with Chris, I think that we both protected that imagery even within our close circles."

Anna Faris Is Focused On Raising Her Son Jack

As previously mentioned, Anna Faris and Chris Pratt have a son together, Jack who was born nine weeks premature. While Pratt has been slammed for seemingly neglecting his son, the actress has always been praised for how she's raising the now nine-year-old. The two are frequently spotted out and about together doing fun activities and Jack also often appears in the actress's social media posts.
