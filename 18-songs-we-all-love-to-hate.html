It’s on heavy rotation, on practically every radio station and even though you claim to hate the song whenever it comes on, you know every single word. You just can’t help singing along to that darn catchy melody and lyrics that are so simple, a chimp could have penned it. This list covers 18 of the most annoying songs you just can’t get out of your head. All with Youtube hits that are so impressive, they look like phone numbers. No judgement from our end if you admittedly enjoy these songs and blast them openly, allowing your inner fan free range. But we totally understand if you’re a closet fan, like many of us are, and only sing along when no one’s watching. Your secret's safe with us.
