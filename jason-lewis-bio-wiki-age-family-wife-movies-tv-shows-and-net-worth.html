Jason Lewis Sex And The City

Jason was playing the role of Smith Jerrod in the Sex and The City film of 2008. Sex and the City was an American romantic comedy-drama television series that was created by Darren Star for HBO. It was an adaptation of Candace Bushnell’s 1997 book of the same name.

The series was premiered in the United States on June 6, 1998, and came to an end on February 22, 2004, with 94 episodes broadcast over six seasons. During its development, the series received contributions from various producers, directors, and screenwriters principally Michael Patrick King.

Sex and the City received both acclaim and criticism for its subjects and characters and is credited with helping to increase HBO’s popularity as a network. The series has won several accolades, including seven of its 54 Emmy Award nominations, eight of its 24 Golden Globe Award nominations, and three of its 11 Screen Actors Guild Award nominations.
