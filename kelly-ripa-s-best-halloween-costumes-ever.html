Kelly Ripa's Best Halloween Costumes Ever!

Publish date: 2024-08-08

Kelly Ripa and her Live cohosts have aced their Halloween episodes over the years with over-the-top costumes and hilarious skits.

For the 2023 holiday, Ripa and husband Mark Consuelos teamed up for multiple couples outfits — even channeling Taylor Swift and Travis Kelce by rocking Kansas City Chiefs gear. The couple, who began hosting the daytime show together in April 2023, also dressed as Vanderpump Rules stars Ariana Madix and Tom Sandoval and recreated Demi Moore and Patrick Swayze‘s iconic pottery scene from the movie Ghost.

Consuelos made frequent appearances on Live before officially becoming a cohost, previously joining his wife for an I Love Lucy-themed costume in 2018.

Ripa has been a mainstay on the talk show since 2001, working alongside Ryan Seacrest, Michael Strahan and the late Regis Philbin through the years. While her cohosts may have changed, her love for Halloween has never wavered.
