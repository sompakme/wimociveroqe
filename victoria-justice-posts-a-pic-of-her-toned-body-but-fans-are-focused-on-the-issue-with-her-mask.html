Victoria Justice Posts A Pic Of Her Toned Body, But Fans Are Focused On The Issue With Her Mask

Publish date: 2024-09-12

It seems as though American actress Victoria Justice has caused made one of the worst social media fumbles you can make during a pandemic.

Just yesterday, the 27 year old took to her Instagram account to share a couple of posts that were meant to show off her toned body and on-point running outfit, only to have them kind of backfire on her.

The Zoey 101 star can be seen posing atop a hill dressed in a white sports bra, bright green exercise leggings, and a black baseball cap seemingly after a hike or a run. In the second photo, Justice makes the 2020 and 2021 fashion faux pas that you should never make: wearing your mask wrong!

Swipe over and the Nickelodeon star can be seen with her mask just under her nose — a major no-no for mask wearing during these times! Fans weren't shy to point it out either, with many jumping at the chance to set her straight and even get into arguments with each other in the comments section.

"Doesn’t even know how to wear a mask. SMH influencers of LA," said one commenter with another chiming in to add, "The mask should cover your mouth and nose". Touche, followers.

The photos are captioned with lyrics to the popular Nas song The World Is Yours and read, "Whose world is this?The world is yours, the world is yours. It's mine, it's mine, it's mine".
