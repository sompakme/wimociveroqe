Sofia Boutella Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-09-01

Sofia Boutella has an estimated net worth of $4 million. This is from her career as a model, actor and dancer. She has starred in several music videos for artists including Jamiroquai, Madonna, Rihanna, Chris Brown, Usher, Ne-Yo, Michael Jackson and more.

Boutella successfully auditioned for the Michael Jackson This Is It concerts but could not attend due to the extension of the Madonna tour, whose dates coincided with the Jackson tour. She was the main character in the music video for Hollywood Tonight by Michael Jackson in February 2011.

In 2017, Boutella portrayed a French secret agent in the David Leitch film Atomic Blonde, which also featured Charlize Theron, James McAvoy, John Goodman and Toby Jones. The same year, she played the titular role in The Mummy, along with Tom Cruise, Russell Crow and Annabelle Wallis.

Boutella played the female lead in SAS: Rogue Heroes, released in 2022.
