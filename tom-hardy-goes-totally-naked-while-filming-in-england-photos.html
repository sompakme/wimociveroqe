Tom Hardy Goes Totally Naked While Filming in England: Photos

Publish date: 2024-10-01

Well, that leaves nothing to the imagination! British hunk Tom Hardy had no qualms about stripping totally buck naked to film scenes for his new miniseries, Taboo, in Essex, England, last week.

The buff 38-year-old movie hero was anything but modest on the closed British film set, when the script called for him to engage in a spot of skinny-dipping in a freezing river.

The Oscar-nominated star, who also bared all in his 2008 movie Bronson, stood on a boat at the river bank and stripped off all his clothes, revealing his buff, muscular body and an array of black, geometric tattoos. 

And rather than call for a body double, or insist on covers or robes until the very last moment, extrovert Hardy seemed happy to strip off, joking and laughing with his crew. And he didn’t even appear in hurry to dress afterward, despite the chilly temperatures!

A well-placed source tells Us Weekly that Hardy refused to wear any sort of cover-up, and even wandered around the closed set in his birthday suit, after he had finished the scene.

And when he spotted a photographer taking snaps, the Mad Max: Fury Road star didn’t freak out. “He just laughed,” our source tells Us.
