Olivia Munn Invested In Start-Ups (And Earned Big Returns)

Munn invested in Uber at a valuation of $290 million which has since grown to $55 billion

Munn is no stranger to being approached for business deals. While some of them are clearly not winners based on their business models, others have potential. As such, Munn has been investing in start-up companies for years, which has been very lucrative for the actor.

Before Uber went public, Munn was approached to invest in the company. While Munn did not know what Uber was, she had a good feeling about it. Listening to her instincts, Munn invested, and that investment paid off in spades.

"When Uber was brought to me I didn’t know what it was," Munn explained in 2019. "I like to go towards brands I feel are disruptive or are creating a new space."

As a result of her investing in the company when it had a $290 million valuation, Munn did quite well for herself. After the company went public, its worth shot through the ceiling, growing to a valuation of over $55 billion.
