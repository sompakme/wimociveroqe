How Does Ben Affleck Spend His Massive Net Worth?

Publish date: 2024-07-29

Ben Affleck has been blowing fans away for over 20 years with his roles in Hollywood blockbusters (The Town, Gone Girl) and less successful pictures as well (Daredevil, Reindeer Games). Whatever projects the Good Will Hunting star chooses, he has certainly been handsomely compensated for his efforts. Indeed, Affleck has amassed a hefty $150 million net worth as of this writing.

With a bank account such as Ben’s, ready to ‘burst at the seams’ as it were, the sky’s the limit as to what the man can buy when he’s out and about. But what does Mr. Affleck like to purchase while perusing the isles? Ben has been known to purchase very extravagant items (an example of such would be his $2500 bathroom rug back in 2012) with his impressive fortune. Let's take a look at what Ben likes to put some of his hard-earned moolah down on.

8 Ben Affleck's Car Collection

Ben has a fondness for slick, curvaceous bodies. Alright, enough of the double entendres. Affleck has amassed a beautiful fleet of luxury automobiles over the years. The Batman V Superman star has collected gems of all shapes, sizes and purposes. Ben’s Lexus RX ($43 thousand) is perfect for driving the kids to school and the like, while the bad boys of his collection such as the Chevrolet Chevelle SS ($58 thousand), the Mercedes-Benz S63 AMG ($151 thousand), and the Audi S8 ($153 thousand) are ideal for speed and style. Ben also happens to care about the environment, so it's only fitting that he has a Tesla Model S ($150 thousand) in his collection.

7 Ben Affleck And Jennifer Garner's $17.55 Million Mansion

Back in 2009, Ben Affleck and Jennifer Garner were still a happily married couple. Together, the couple purchased the Los Angeles mansion for $17 million. Before completion of the luxury estate, Affleck was with Garner on the east coast. Garner finished a promotional tour for her film Ghosts of Girlfriends Past, while Affleck wrapped filming of The Company Man. Upon wrapping, the couple flew back to L.A. where their finished estate greeted them with open arms… err, doors.

6 A Pacific Palisades Mansion That Cost $19.25 Million

How much space does one need? 12,853-square-feet? That’s how much Affleck’s Pacific Palisades mansion covers. Coming in at a hefty $19 million and change, Ben’s palace in the Palisades is massive, luxurious and close to the mother of his children. Indeed, Jennifer Garner is but a mile away, which surely makes co-parenting much easier when not having to traverse too far. Affleck’s property covers nearly one-third of an acre and boasts seven bedrooms and six full and two half bathrooms spread out over three levels.

5 Ben Affleck's BMW S1000RR Motorcycle

When speeding around the streets of Los Angeles, Ben can be seen piloting his BMW S1000RR. The red, white and blue bike comes in at $16 thousand and change. This is pretty much pocket money for a man worth over $100 million. With a maximum torque of 83 lb-ft at 11,000 rpm and a torque curve of at least 74 lb-ft over a range of 5,500 to 14,500 (rpm), Affleck can speed up and down Hollywood Boulevard in style. Affleck does have a noteworthy custom BMW in his arsenal, but that was a gift and thus doesn't count.

4 Ben Affleck's ‘Big House’ In Savannah

Located in beautiful (albeit muggy) Savannah, Ben purchased ‘The Big House’ back in 2003, while ‘Bennifer’ round 1 was in effect. The breathtaking Hampton Island residence spans 87 acres and is built in the style of an old plantation house. The three-home property originally cost the Justice League actor $7.1 million; however, Affleck is in the process of selling the estate for $8.9 million, even slashing the price to ensure a sale.

3 $45 Thousand Foundrae Necklace For Jennifer Lopez

‘Bennifer’ 2.1 is upon us and despite the couple's quirks (such as engaging in PDA while masked and J-Lo posing for selfies inside Affleck's car), fans have embraced the couple once again. With the reformation of the extravagant couple comes extravagant gift-giving, and Ben certainly delivered with a $45 thousand Foundrae necklace he gifted J-Lo. The necklace includes key and heart pendants, as well as charms that symbolize a compass, karma, passion. Happy Birthday, indeed.

2 $1.2 Million Pink Diamond Engagement Ring For Jennifer Lopez

With the reformation of ‘Bennifer’ being in full effect, there have been reports of Affleck browsing at rings for J-Lo. Jennifer Lopez is no stranger to the prospect of engagement with Mr. Affleck; back in the early 2000s, the “Jenny From the Block” singer was gifted a $1.2 million pink diamond engagement ring when Ben first proposed. With Ben now looking through the jewelry aisles, it may be only a matter of time before he pops the question once again.

1 $85 Million Mansion (With Jennifer Lopez)

Ben certainly seems to have an affinity for real estate and once again the Pearl Harbor actor, along with J-Lo, has his eyes on another stunning property. Located in Beverly Hills, this beautiful 38,000 square foot mansion covers 5.2 acres and features an indoor sports complex with basketball, pickleball, boxing ring, and a complete gym. With 12 bedrooms, 24 bathrooms and a caretaker as well as guard house, this house is more of a modern-day castle than a house.
