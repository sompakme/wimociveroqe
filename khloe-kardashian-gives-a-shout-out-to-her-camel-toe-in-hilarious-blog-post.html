Khloe Kardashian Gives a Shout Out to Her Camel Toe in Hilarious Blog Post

Publish date: 2024-07-23

Khloé Kardashian has never been shy about her camel toe. The reality star, who frequently alludes to her lady parts on Keeping Up With the Kardashians and Kocktails With Khloé, reminisced about her most revealing looks in a post on her website on Friday, March 4.

The youngest Kardashian sister, 31, documented “Camille the Camel’s Top 9 Fashion Moments” in a new post on the site’s Koko’s Closet section.

“There’s no hiding it: I have a puffy p—y, people!!!” she wrote. “Ain’t no shame in the came toe game, LOL. Mine has a name: Camille the Camel! She steals the spotlight whenever I wear tight jeans, so I had to count down her top fashion moments, haha. Give me a break, people! Fashion doesn’t need to be so serious all the time!”

Kardashian jokingly added, “Move over #FreeTheNipple, it’s all about #FreeTheCamelToe!!!”

The Kocktails With Khloé host attached photos of nine of her favorite “full-frontal toe” moments from the past decade.

“YASSS, Camille!!!” she continued. “Helping me kill the fashion game since Day 1! Haha, hope you guys enjoyed this camel countdown as much as I did.”
