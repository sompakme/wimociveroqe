Lil Kim Brings Out Sukihana At ONE Music Fest: This Means So Much

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Lil Kim took the stage at ONE Music Fest in Atlanta over the weekend, where she brought out a special guest.

“F**k it, I’ma bring my sister out here on some freaky sh*t,” the Queen Bee teased the crowd as Sukihana joined the stage to roaring applause. The provocative rapper then performed her viral song, “Eating.”

Suki shared her gratitude online, as she expressed how much the moment meant to her.

“@lilkimthequeenbee brought me outtttt,” she wrote on Instagram. “This means so much to [bee emoji] my hard work is paying off. I am actually just getting started. So excited to show the world SUKIHANA. This is a dream come true for me.”

As a vet in Hip-Hop, Kim has continued to show love to rising stars over the years like Suki, Lola Brooke, Cardi B and more. However, there’s one woman rapper who has felt indifferent about some of the newer rap girls.

Khia recently vented on how she’s “tired” of fans comparing her to current rappers like Sexyy Red, City Girls, Suki and others.

“These hoes was raised by Trina…” she started in an IG video. “I’m about tired of y’all comparing me to these h*es. ‘It’s respect me, it’s snatch the motherf**in’ cat back. It’s don’t trust no motherf*ckin’ ni**a get your own sh*t…’ It ain’t no ‘eat no ‘ni**a’s a** and suck no ni**a’s toes.'” The “My Neck, My Back,” rhymer was referring to the lyrics of Sukihana’s aforementioned song, “Eating,” where she raps: “Eatin’ his a**, eatin’ a ni**a a**/ Eatin’ a ni**a as**, we be eatin’ ni**as a**.”

Khia also vaguely called out recent incidents like Sexyy Red having her sex tape leaked on her Instagram Story, as well as Sukihana’s pictures of her nude body surfacing on the Internet.

The Miami artist responded to Khia’s sentiments with, “I will knock Khia tf out. SUKIHANA is SUKIHANA, a has-been is a has-been. I’m booked every weekend until next September of NEXT YEAR. I get it I’m ratchet but I live in my truth. Atlease I don’t sit in front of a library mad at Trina everyday with 172 teeth in my mouth.”
