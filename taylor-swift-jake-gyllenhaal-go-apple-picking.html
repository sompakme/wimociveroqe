Taylor Swift, Jake Gyllenhaal Go Apple Picking

Publish date: 2024-09-09

Saturday Night Live wasn’t Taylor Swift and Jake Gyllenhaal‘s first outing together this past weekend.

The two were spotted Saturday at the Fishkill Farms in Hopewell Junction, N.Y, where they toured fall foliage and went apple-picking, reports the new Us Weekly (on newsstands Wednesday).

“They were walking through the trees, having fun together,” says a local. “They looked happy.”

Adds the witness, “Some of the customers tried to take a photo of them, but they were hiding from them in the trees. They drove through the orchard and bought the apples on the way out.” (Gyllenhaal, 29, paid the $3 tab.)
