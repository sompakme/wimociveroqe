American actress Yolanda Pecoraro has an estimated net worth of $1 million. She earned her net worth when she appeared in several films and as a model. Born on February 28, 1985 in New Jersey, Yolie, as she is sometimes called, her mother is from Nicaragua and her father is of Italian descent. At a very young age, she and her sisters were very passionate artists who have created paintings and drawings as well as writing stories and making short films. She moved to Los Angeles as a young adult and enrolled in an acting class to pursue a career in film.

As a professional model, Yolie made her debut in her appearance in the covers of various magazines including Open Your Eyes and Elite Traveler. She has been featured in a special issue of Maxim, listing her among the top Latin celebrities. She also appeared in print and commercial campaigns for Verizon, Ford, Target, Nokia, Sunkist, Toyota and Pioneer.

She appeared in the movie The Ugly Truth (2009) with Katherine Heigl and Gerard Butler, Greek, Once Fallen (2009) starring Ed Harris, Death Calls (2009), and the television series Online Gamer. She recently made headlines as the rumored woman who has been helping Tom Cruise to move past his divorce from Katie Holmes.
