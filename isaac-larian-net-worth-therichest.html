Isaac Larian

Birthname Isaac Larian Birthdate March 28, 1954 Birthplace Kashan, Iran Education California State University, Los Angeles Profession Businessman Net worth $1,200,000,000 Source of Wealth Toys, MGA Entertainment Nationality American Country United States Marital Status Married (Angela Neman) Children 3 (Cameron, Jason, Jasmin)

Isaac Larian is an American businessman with an estimated net worth of $1.2 billion according to Forbes List of Billionaires in 2014. He currently serves as the Chief Executive Officer of the MGA Entertainment.

Originating from Kashan, Iran, Isaac Larian was born on March 28, 1954. At age 17, he moved to the United States. Eventually, he attended the University of California, Los Angeles where he finished a degree in Civil Engineering. From a mere electronic good exporting company, his business expanded to the development, creation, and marketing of Bratz doll.

Larian is currently married to Angela Neman with whom he has three children named Cameron, Jason, and Jasmin. Both Cameron and Jasmin already bear the names of Larian's Bratz dolls.

In 2004, Isaac Larian was chosen as the recipient of the Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year Award for the category of consumer products. He was also named as the overall Ernst & Young Entrepreneur of the Year three years later.
