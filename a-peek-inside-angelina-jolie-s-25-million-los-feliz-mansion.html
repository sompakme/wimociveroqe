Join us as we enter Angelina Jolie's opulent world, revealing the captivating attraction of her unique $25 million property in Los Feliz, California. This lavish mansion, initially owned by artist Cecil B. DeMille, bears witness to Jolie's new beginnings upon her divorce from Brad Pitt. The estate, built in 1913, measures 7,500 square feet across 2.1 acres and provides an insight into the actress's lavish lifestyle. The interior oozes timeless charm, with six bedrooms, ten bathrooms, a library, a fine kitchen, and more. A landscaped garden and an outdoor terrace embody Jolie's enduring star power and superb taste.
