PARTYNEXTDOOR Announces Headlining Tour Dates

Publish date: 2024-08-05

PARTYNEXTDOOR is bringing the party to you on his forthcoming, headlining North American tour this fall. OVO Sounds’ resident crooner will hit the stage in 14 cities, beginning with Le Belmont in Montreal, QC on Oct. 19 and wrapping up at The Roxy in Los Angeles on Oct. 22. The outing comes after his eight-day stint on the the “Drake Vs. Lil Wayne” tour. Tickets will hit the Internet on Sept. 18 for select dates (*) and Sept. 19. View his itinerary and lay back to the sounds of Party below.
