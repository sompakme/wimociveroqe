The Closest Friendships Formed On The Show

Publish date: 2024-08-24

With the season finale wrapped up and the reunion on the way, season 2 of Love Is Blind has shown some rocky relationships. Netflix has begun releasing episodes week by week for some of their new shows, and it kept fans hanging on for each new episode for more drama. Five couples made it through to the season finale where the weddings begin. Two couples say 'I do,' while three people were rejected at the altar. This season had plenty of ups and downs, from horrifying comments about others' physical appearance to multiple love triangles interfering with the couples' attempts at a healthy relationship. With all the emotional distress going on this season, lots of enemies were made, along with a few friendships.

Many tears were shed throughout the season, but we saw lots of laughs between cast members as well. Whether they found love or not, many found long-lasting friendships. This show takes a toll on cast members' mental health while trying to find love, and many found strong friendships that lasted months after filming the show.

6 Danielle And Deepti Became Best Friends

Fans' favorite duo from Love Is Blind: Season 2 is Danielle and Deepti. The two ladies went on the show to find love and found a best friend within each other. Danielle has received a lot of hate, especially on her Instagram page, since the show aired. Deepti understands Danielle's very open discussion about mental health issues and has continuously supported Danielle in her struggles. Although many fans view Danielle's behavior on the show as gaslighting and projecting her insecurities onto Nick, others see her as a mental health advocate and praise her for raising awareness. Whichever way someone sees it, Deepti has been emphasizing the importance of kindness and asking fans to stop leaving hate comments on Danielle's Instagram account. Deepti talks a lot about how awesome her new best friend is, regardless of how she was portrayed.

5 Sentimental Posts About True Friendship

The strongest friendship formed on the show is by far Danielle and Deepti. The two girls had different outcomes at the finale but have been supporting each other through it all. On both Danielle and Deepti's Instagram accounts, they have shared emotional posts about how strong the other friend is. Danielle gets married after an emotional rollercoaster with Nick, while Deepti excites fans when she stands up Shake at the altar after a season of his horrible comments. The two friends helped each other through the process and have stayed close ever since. Deepti was engaged to a man who publicly humiliated her throughout the entire season, while Danielle has received horrific backlash for her mental health struggles on the show, and the two ladies have been each other's number one supporter since the beginning.

4 Shaina Loves Deepti Too, But Is It Mutual?

Shaina isn't much of a fan favorite, but she did take to Instagram to show her love for Deepti. Who isn't best friends with Deepti after this season?! Deepti shares some love with Shaina on Instagram, but it's still debatable if Shaina left the show with any friends. Shaina accepted Kyle's proposal, and while being engaged, still tried to win over Shayne. On the "Viall Files" podcast, hosted by Nick Viall, who appeared on The Bachelor and two seasons of The Bachelorette, Shaina reveals that she is in a serious relationship. Fans will have to wait until the reunion to find out if Shaina truly has any friends left after the drama she caused on this season.

3 Possibly Two Happily Married Couples

Only two out of the five couples had both partners say 'I do' at the altar: Iyanna and Jarrette and Danielle and Nick. While Iyanna and Jarrette's relationship seems more easy-going and happy, fans are waiting for the reunion to see if they are still together. Danielle and Nick, on the other han, shocked fans when they both said 'I do'.
