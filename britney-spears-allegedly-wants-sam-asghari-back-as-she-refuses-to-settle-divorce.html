Britney Spears Allegedly Wants Sam Asghari Back As She Refuses To Settle Divorce

Publish date: 2024-08-26

There are few details known about Britney Spears and Sam Asghari’s marriage, other than the Iranian native filed for divorce in August 2023 after only 14 months of marriage, despite being together for years. Nearly a year later, the estranged couple are still trying to settle their divorce, as reports claim Sam is hoping to get more money than the prenup entitles him to.

Now, a new report is providing insight into the final few weeks of the couple’s marriage before Sam filed to dissolve it, and it sounds like things were chaotic, adding context to why things imploded.

Sam And Britney Apparently Only Talk Through Lawyers Now

Speaking to OK! Magazine, a source claims that Sam’s decision to leave the marriage wasn’t exactly surprising, given the dynamic between the couple in the weeks leading up to his divorce filing. They described the relationship as “tumultuous” and adds they “only talk through lawyers.”

The source claims that Britney misses Sam, implying the pop star may regret past behavior that pushed her husband to choose divorce. “[She] wants Sam back badly,” they said.

"She misses the fun they used to have together," they said. "She wishes she could pick up the phone and convince Sam to give her another chance, but things are way too messy between them right now."

Britney Has Admitted To Missing Her Ex-Husband Before

The source’s sentiments seemed true earlier this month, when Spears shared a video of her and Asghari dancing together on her social media. The clip has since been removed.

"The time he picked me up… It’s weird cause there are always strange turns for relationships with friends family or your loved ones in the [rollercoaster emoji] of any journey you’re on with someone!!!" Britney wrote via Instagram. "Looking back is hard sometimes but it’s honestly crucial … I’m honestly too sensitive in most situations.”

Britney continued, "I speak about my past sometimes because I want to protect myself from the same mistakes as before!!! What I usually do if I’m going through too much is shut down but I sometimes lose the ability to figure out what’s going on or how I feel at all!!!"
