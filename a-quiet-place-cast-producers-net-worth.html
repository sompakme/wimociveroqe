A Quiet Place: Cast &amp; Producers' Net Worth

A Quiet Place is a riveting science fiction horror film that, when you read what it’s about, doesn’t sound like it would be all that compelling. But watch it and you’re totally captivated. Why might you not initially be convinced? The film has no dialogue. Directed by and starring John Krasinski, it’s about a family living in a post-apocalyptic world where, if you make even the slightest sound, blind aliens with a very sharp sense of hearing will attack and kill you.

Released in 2018, the film features only a handful of actors, including Krasinski, Emily Blunt, who plays his wife (and to whom he’s married in real life), and their three kids. But it also has some mega producing power behind it. How much are they all worth? Have a look at these figures from, unless otherwise stated, CelebrityNetWorth.com.

Millicent Simmonds – Under Review

Playing Regan, the eldest child and only daughter of the couple, the character is deaf just like Simmonds in real life. This gives her particular insight into the new world since she understands living in a quiet place because, well, that was her everyday reality before the world came to an end.

The teenage girl has appeared in a few other projects, including Wonderstruck, Ani Mack, and This Close. And she has been tapped to reprise her role in A Quiet Place: Part II, which is scheduled to release in 2020. But her net worth is still under review.

Cade Woodward – Approx. $1-5 million

A child actor, Woodward got his first acting gig in this movie, playing the youngest of the couple’s three children, Beau. After that, he snagged a role in one of the biggest movies of the decade, Avengers: Endgame. He played Nathaniel Barton, the third child of Clint Barton (Hawkeye) and his wife Laura. His character was killed off in Thanos’ snap but resurrected again by Hulk in 2023.

Despite only just starting to act, Trendcelebsnow.com reports that Woodward might already be worth at least a million thanks to his involvement in two massive box office successes.

Noah Jupe – Approx. $2 million

As Marcus, the eldest son, Jupe was cast in the movie after Krasinski saw his performance in the miniseries The Night Manager as well as early screenings of the 2017 film Suburbicon. Jupe also appeared in Wonder, Ford v Ferrari, and Holmes and Watson.

Born in London, the 14-year-old is the son of a filmmaker and actress, so it’s no wonder he pursued acting. He got his start in 2015/2016 appearing in Penny Dreadful and Downton Abbey. You can see him more recently in the independent film Honey Boy, based on Shia LaBeouf’s life, where he plays a younger version of LaBeouf. His approximated net worth is $2 million according to Superbhub.com.

Andrew Form - $5 million

Listed as a producer for the movie, Form has produced plenty of other films in the horror genre like Friday the 13th and The Purge, as well as Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles. He, along Michael Bay and Brad Fuller, co-founded the company Platinum Dunes.

Married to actress Jordana Brewster, the couple has two children together. His next project will be a sequel to The Purge, which will be released sometime in 2020 and has yet to be named. (Photo: Splashnewsonline)

Leon Russom – Approx. $15 million

According to Allstarbio.com, Russom has amassed a pretty nice net worth throughout his lengthy career. He plays a mysterious man in the woods that the family comes across in the film, and he is best known for his work in soap operas like Guiding Light as well as for portraying Admiral Toddman in the Starfleet Commander-in-Chief in different Star Trek episodes.

He has also racked up various TV series appearances, including on Bones, Jericho, Prison Break, and Cold Case. He's had small parts in The Big Lebowski and True Grit. The 78-year-old is also a stage actor.

Emily Blunt - $25 million

An accomplished actor with lots of credits to her name, including The Devil Wears Prada, The Adjustment Bureau, Into the Woods, The Girl on the Train, Sicario, and Mary Poppins Returns, Blunt has been married to Krasinski since 2010 and the couple has two children together. In the movie, she plays the mother, Evelyn, who wants her children to grow up strong and independent, despite the circumstances of the world.

She will reprise her role for the sequel. Other upcoming projects include co-starring with Dwayne Johnson in Jungle Cruise and with Jamie Dornan in the romantic drama Wild Mountain Thyme.

John Krasinski - $30 million

Playing the father Lee, an engineer and survivalist who spends each day trying to get his family through to the next, Krasinski rose to fame following his role on the NBC sitcom The Office. He started producing and directing during the later seasons of that show as well.

Married to Blunt, he has appeared in many other films, including License to Wed, It’s Complicated, and Leatherheads. He can be seen now playing the title character in Amazon Prime Video Series Tom Clancy's Jack Ryan, which he also produced. He does voice work as well and owns his own production company Sunday Night Productions.

Brad Fuller - $50 million

As one of the three executive producers of the movie, Fuller is co-owner of Platinum Dunes. Childhood friends with Michael Bay, the trio specialized in horror films when they first got together in the early 2000s. Some of the films under his name include The Texas Chainsaw Massacre and The Amityville Horror.

He has also produced TV projects including Black Sails, Billion Dollar Wreck, and The Last Ship, and is a producer for Tom Clancy’s Jack Ryan, in which Krasinski stars.

Michael Bay - $430 million

One of the most well-known and successful filmmakers, producers, and directors of this generation, Bay is often associated with big-budget and high-concept films like Armageddon, Pearl Harbor, and the Transformers series.

As a producer of this film alongside his two partners at Platinum Dunes, he is one of the most commercially successful directors in history – his films have cumulatively grossed more than $7.8 billion worldwide. So it’s no surprise that Bay has the highest net worth of anyone involved in this film and soon, its sequel.
