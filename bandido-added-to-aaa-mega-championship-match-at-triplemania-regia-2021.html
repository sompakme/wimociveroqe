Bandido Added To AAA Mega Championship Match At TripleMania Regia 2021

Publish date: 2024-07-28

It appears is going out of its way to make up for Kenny Omega not being cleared to compete at TripleMania Regia this year.

Since the announcement that Omega would need to forfeit the AAA Mega Championship due to injury, AAA has been putting together a star-studded main event to crown a new champion.

AAA Director-General Dorian Joaquín Roldán Peña took to social media this morning to announce the addition of Ring of Honor World Champion Bandido to the AAA Mega Championship match, making it a fatal-five-way:

“The fifth and last challenger for the MegaCampeonato of @luchalibreaaa; arrives from @ringofhonor. @bandidowrestler

The final fight is: @vikingo_aaa vs @gloat vs @TheLethalJay vs @theBobbyFish vs @bandidowrestler

Good luck and welcome to #TriplemaniaRegia; soon more news,” Dorian Joaquín Roldán Peña tweeted this morning.

Here is the current card for TripleMania Regia 2021:

  • El Hijo del Vikingo vs. Samuray Del Sol vs. Jay Lethal vs. Bobby Fish vs. Bandido for the vacant AAA Mega Championship
  • Cain Velasquez, Pagano, and Psycho Clown vs. L.A. Park, Rey Escorpión, and Taurus
  • FTR will defend the AAA World Tag Team Championships against The Lucha Bros
  • Dave The Clown, Monster Clown, and Murder Clown vs. DMT Azul, Puma King, and Sam Adonis
  • Dragon Lee and Dralístico vs. Laredo Kid and TBA
  • Abismo Negro Jr., Arez, and Psicosis vs. Carta Brava Jr., Mocho Cota Jr., and Tito Santana vs. Cuatrero, Forastero, and Sansón in a three-way trios match
  • Engañso and Venenoide vs. Gran Mazo and Leyenda Americana in a Marvel Lucha Libre tag match
  • Faby Apache, Lady Shani, and Sexy Star vs. Flammer, La Hiedra, and Lady Maravilla in a trios tag match
