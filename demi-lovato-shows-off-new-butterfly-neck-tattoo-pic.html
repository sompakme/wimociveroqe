Demi Lovato Shows Off New Butterfly Neck Tattoo: Pic

Publish date: 2024-08-29

Demi Lovato has a new neck tattoo! The 28-year old unveiled the new ink on Wednesday, September 2, via an Instagram post — and fans are anxious to learn more about the design.

In the selfie, the Disney Channel alum poses in a cleavage-baring floral dress designed by Alexandre Vauthier Official, an Elise Paige Jewels necklace and pink pointy Casadei pumps. She tilts her head to one side to snap the pic, showing off the large butterfly tattoo on the right side of her neck.

The hitmaker captioned the stunning snap, “Dear little me, I’m sorry that it took so long but baby your free..🥰☺️💖.” Fans are speculating that these are new song lyrics, but others are more focused on the new ink.

“THAT BUTTERFLY TATTOO THOUGH,” one fan tweeted and another wrote, “Girl is that a butterfly tattoo I see? That looks gorgeous as f–k!”

The “Anyone” singer has an impressive tattoo collection that’s complete with over 25 different designs. She already has two other neck designs, including the word “survivor” and an elaborate “divine feminine’ tattoo, both of which she unveiled in December 2019.

Both of those neck designs are symbolic of Lovato’s journey to recovery. The “Survivor” ink, brought to life by celeb-inker Dr. Woo, represents how far she’s come in her sobriety journey following her near-fatal overdose in 2018.

And the intricately designed “divine feminine” tattoo was the work of Rome-based tattoo artist Alessandro Capozzi. The star posted a black-and-white photo of design with a lengthy caption detailing its significance.
