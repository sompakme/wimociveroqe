What is Mischa Barton doing these days?

Publish date: 2024-08-31

British-American actress, Mischa Barton has recently been announced as a guest star on the reboot Australian popular soap opera, Neighbours. The announcement was made on Monday, April 17, 2023.

“I’m excited to be part of this iconic show’s next chapter, and I am really looking forward to being back in Australia – a place I know and love,” she said.

Executive Producer of the show, Jason Herbison also shared;

“We are beyond excited to have an actor of Mischa’s caliber join us for this exciting new chapter. Mischa’s character is dynamic and unpredictable, and will have an instant presence on Ramsay Street. Our loyal viewers are going to love her, locally, and abroad.”

Barton’s first film appearance was in the movie Lawn Dogs. She went ahead to star in several movies and tv shows including Notting Hill (1999), The Sixth Sense (1999), All My Children (1996), Lost and Delirious (2001), and more.

She is a recipient of Two Teen Choice Awards which she won in 2004 and 2006 for Choice Breakout TV Star – Female and Choice TV Actress, respectively.
