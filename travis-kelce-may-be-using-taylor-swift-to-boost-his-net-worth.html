Travis Kelce May Be Using Taylor Swift To Boost His Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-07-10

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift's new love interest Travis Kelce has experienced a surge in popularity and jersey sales since being linked to the pop star.
  • Kelce's NFL jerseys were among the top five best-selling in the last 24 hours, with a remarkable 400% increase in sales.
  • There are rumors that Kelce may be using Swift to boost his career and net worth, with his ex-girlfriend warning Swift of his potential ulterior motives.

Though Taylor Swift seems enamored with her new love interest Travis Kelce, there’s concern he may be using her to boost his career and ultimately his personal net worth. There’s no denying that Travis’ popularity has skyrocketed since being linked to the pop star earlier this month. Particularly, his jersey sales have spiked in demand.

As per a TMZ Sports report, Travis Kelce jerseys sales are experiencing a surge following Sunday's game. Fanatics, the NFL’s official e-commerce partner, informed TMZ that Kelce's NFL jerseys ranked among the top five best-selling in the last 24 hours, with a remarkable 400% in sales during that timeframe.

"Yesterday, Travis Kelce was one of the top 5 selling NFL players and saw a nearly 400% spike in sales throughout the Fanatics network of sites, including NFLShop.com," Fanatics explained to the publication.

Though it’s unclear exactly what drove the spike in purchases, the surge in jersey sales coincides with Taylor's visit to Arrowhead Stadium to witness the Chiefs' commanding 41-10 victory over the Bears, where she was seen bonding with Travis’ mom while cheering him on.

While the pair seem to be going strong, there’s more adding fuel to the rumors that Travis is hoping for a career boost thanks to Taylor - Travis’ ex-girlfriend Maya Benberry recently issued a public warning to the singer, suggesting, Travis could be dating her mainly for clout.

“Taylor seems like such a fun girl with a beautiful spirit so I wish her the best of luck but I wouldn’t be a girls' girl if I didn’t advise her to be smart!” Maya told Daily Mail. “I’m sure by now she has mastered the ability to see who is really there for her - and who is just using her,” she went on. “I can only hope that she comes out the teacher in this situation and not the student.”

Even more, Maya implied that Tavis has a history of infidelity in relationships. “The man that I knew him to be when we dated,” she continued. “Only time will tell but like the saying always goes, once a cheater, always a cheater.”

Maya and Travis' romantic relationship lasted a mere two months after she emerged as the winner on "Catching Kelce," a reality show featuring 49 other contestants representing all 50 states competing for the affections of the NFL player. Following their breakup in 2016, Maya posted a series of tweets, which have since been deleted, implying that Travis had been unfaithful to her with sports broadcaster Kayla Nicole, who he dated up until last year.
