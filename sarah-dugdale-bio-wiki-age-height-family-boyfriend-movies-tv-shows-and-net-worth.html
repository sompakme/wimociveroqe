Career

Dugdale began her acting career in 2016, with a guest role on the TV series “Supernatural”. She then went on to appear in several other TV shows, including “The Haunting Hour”, “The Secret Circle”, and “Arrow”.

Dugdale has also had a successful career in film. In 2018, she starred in the horror film “The Bad Seed”. She then went on to appear in the thriller films “In the Shadow of the Moon” and “Death of a Cheerleader” in 2019.

In 2021, she starred in the Netflix horror film “There’s Someone Inside Your House”. She most recently starred in the 2023 film “Wonders of the Invisible World Revealed”.
