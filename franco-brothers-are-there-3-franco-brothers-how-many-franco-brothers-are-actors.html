Franco Brothers: Are There 3 Franco Brothers? How Many Franco Brothers Are Actors?

Publish date: 2024-07-18

The Franco Brothers are three from the same family-friendly James Franco, Dave Franco and Tom Franco.

James Franco

James Franco, the older Franco brother is a Hollywood actor, writer and filmmaker. He was born on April 19, 1978.

Actor James Franco came into limelight with the movie “Freaks and Geeks” and later went on to win an award for his roles in “127 Hours,” “Milk,” “Pineapple Express” and many more.

Tom Franco

Thomas Franco was born on April 14, 1980. Tom is also an actor, and the founder of the Firehouse Art Collective in Berkeley, California.

He first appeared in the comedy horror film Basket Case 2 (1990), and went ahead to starr in A Peace of History (2005) and The Devil Wears a Toupee (2007).
