Patrick Mahomes Praises Wife Brittany Mahomes Despite What Taylor Swift Fans May Think Of Her

Publish date: 2024-09-21

Highlights

  • Brittany Mahomes recently created controversy with her old tweets about Taylor Swift, but husband Patrick Mahomes is still her biggest fan.
  • Patrick praises his wife Brittany as his best friend and says she supports his football career.
  • Brittany and Patrick Mahomes' relationship flourishes, and continues to grow stronger with milestones.

Brittany Mahomes has made headlines recently for a negative reason: the nasty tweets she made about Taylor Swift resurfaced. What she said when she was a teenager came back to bite her, and fans have a lot of thoughts about this, considering the two women appear to be great friends now (although it has been reported that Swift is not concerned with the comments and has forgiven Brittany). Despite what Swifties may or may not think about her, there is one person who is still her biggest fan and that is her husband, Patrick Mahomes.

"People don't even realize how much she does, taking care of the day-to-day stuff and making it so where I can focus on football, focus on my craft and everything like that," he said on Logan Paul's podcast, Impaulsive. He referred to Brittany as "a half-of-fame mom" and "a hall-of-fame wife."

What Did Patrick Mahomes Say About Brittany Mahomes?

In the same interview, Patrick Mahomes praised Brittany Mahomes and called her his "best friend." He said, "It makes it a lot easier. ... When you get to come home and your best friend's there, and you can just hang out, it makes you want to be there all the time. She pushes me to be great, and she's done a lot of great things herself."

But fans of the couple, especially those who have kept up with them from the beginning, will not be surprised by Mahomes' latest comments about his wife. His social media (and hers, too) are filled with sweet pictures of them together, and the captions on their posts often provide a glimpse into their relationship. This includes a time when Brittany captioned a picture of herself and Patrick with the message, "Thank God for giving me you." Patrick has also praised his wife publicly on multiple occasions.

Brittany Mahomes And Patrick Mahomes' Relationship Continues To Blossom

Brittany Mahomes and Patrick Mahomes have one of the sweetest relationships. It started when they were at Whitehouse High School in Texas. The couple has been dating since 2012, and their relationship is still going strong. They grew together despite attending different colleges, and in 2020, they got engaged. That same year, they also announced that Brittany was pregnant with their first child, Sterling.

On March 12, 2022, the couple celebrated another milestone with their wedding in Maui, Hawaii. Then, just two months later, they announced they would be expecting their second child. There is no denying they are incredibly in love, and Patrick has spoken with great affection about his wife. In an earlier interview with People, he said, "I have a great wife. I think that helps out a ton. She helps me out a ton by taking stuff off my plate." He continued, "I have great people around me and I can be Patrick Mahomes. I can be myself, the same person I've grown up being — and luckily that's won a couple of Super Bowls."
