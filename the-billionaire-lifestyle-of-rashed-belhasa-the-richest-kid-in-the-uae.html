The Billionaire Lifestyle Of Rashed Belhasa: The Richest Kid In The UAE

Dubai is home to a host of influential aristocrats that live an affluent lifestyle. The cosmopolitan metropolis is a popular tourist destination and features a vast number of five-star hotels as well as the tallest building in the world, Burj Khalifa. From fancy cars to ludicrously expensive real estate, Dubai oozes panache. A few prominent personalities in the invigorating city take the extravagance factor to a whole new level.

Also known as the richest kid in the UAE, Rashed is the son of Dubai construction billionaire Saif Ahmed Belhasa. The influential youngster has established his own e-commerce store and is also the co-owner of a men's streetwear line known as 'KA-1'. The affluent young entrepreneur has a large social media following, with his Instagram handle and popular Youtube channel 'Money Kicks' giving the world a glimpse into the extravagant and mirthful lifestyle of the passionate billionaire.

Rashed doesn't shy away from displaying his extravagant lifestyle, as the thrill-seeking youngster spends his immense fortune on exorbitant automobiles, top-of-the-line air travel, and a varied range of sneakers that ooze panache.

His Enormous Fan Following

Social media is the ultimate tool to expand business ventures and consumer connect, and UAE's richest kid is undeniably at the top of his game in this regard. Rashed's fan following is enormous and rather diverse, with his Instagram and Youtube accounts featuring more than 5 million followers when combined.

The young businessman spends his valuable time interacting with some of the world's most influential social media personalities like Mohamed Beiraghdary from 'Mo vlogs', big-time Bollywood actors like Salman Khan, and even critically acclaimed musicians like Rihanna, making him one of the most prominent well-connected entrepreneurs in the world.

His Million-Dollar Sneaker Collection

Rashed's passion for collecting some of the world's most expensive and customized sneakers is undeniably unique. The young billionaire is the proud owner of some of the most desirable kicks ever made, with his somewhat unorthodox area of interest featured on an episode of the popular television series 'World's Weirdest Homes'.

The diverse collection features the likes of ultra-rare Air Jordans, swanky Nikes, and uber-stylish Adidas Yeezys that are sure to put a smile on the faces of sneakerheads the world over. The most prominent pair of kicks in his mesmerizing collection are the game-worn soccer cleats from Manchester United midfielder Paul Pogba, with only 14 pairs of this priceless piece of footgear ever made.

His Exhilarating Range Of Automobiles

Rashed's varied range of exorbitant automobiles exemplifies his affluent lifestyle. The young entrepreneur's garage is home to some of the world's fastest supercars and opulent luxury cruisers that is sure to raise the excitement levels of motoring enthusiasts the world over.

The stunning collection includes the uber-stylish and blisteringly quick 6.5-Liter V12 Lamborghini Aventador that costs a whopping $500,000, as well as a customized 6.3-Liter V12 Ferrari F12 Berlinetta that is wrapped in Supreme and Louis Vuitton logos, costing an eye-watering $400,000.

Rashed's personalized garage exudes an element of diversity, with the $330,000 ultra-luxurious Rolls-Royce Wraith catering to the young entrepreneur's need for comfort and opulence. The Wraith is one of Rolls-Royce's most versatile luxury cruisers, with its 6.6-Liter V12 powermill giving it supercar-like performance while delivering a cloud-like riding experience as expected from a Rolls-Royce.

His Exorbitant Residence

Rashed's private residence features a customized game room, mesmerizing views, and even a professional-looking football pitch, making it one of the most spacious and expensive abodes in Dubai. The influential billionaire's luxurious abode is doubly special, as it also comprises a humongous farm that is home to over 500 exotic animals.

Rashed's passion for animals is evident, as his personalized farm features the likes of Bengal tigers, zebras, lions, giraffes, cheetahs, and even chimpanzees, making it one of the most unorthodox yet immensely desirable pieces of real estate in the world.

Rashed Saif Belhasa is the epitome of extravagance. The young billionaire's rise to the top encompasses passion, desire, and unparalleled levels of innovation, as the intelligent entrepreneur has flawlessly capitalized on his 'rich-kid' image by showcasing his ever-so-interesting everyday lifestyle to the world through the help of social media. This intelligent business decision has helped Rashed create an identity of his own, with his enormous $100 million fortune proving the young entrepreneur's passionate outlook on life. There's no looking back for the affluent young billionaire, as his extravagant lifestyle will only get that much more enriching in the near future.
