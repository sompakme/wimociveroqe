The Surf Band Pro Product

The Surf Band Pro is a wristband device designed for surfers, beachgoers, and outdoor enthusiasts. This innovative product allows users to store essential gels and lotions such as sunscreen, sanitizer, and insect repellent in a convenient and accessible manner. With the Surf Band Pro, you can keep your hands free while ensuring that your necessary products are always within reach.

Designed to provide maximum functionality and comfort, the Surf Band Pro features an adjustable Velcro wrist strap that ensures a secure and custom fit for users of all sizes. The band is made from durable and waterproof materials, making it suitable for use in various outdoor activities.

Equipped with a refillable reservoir, the Surf Band Pro allows for easy and hygienic storage of gels and lotions. The simple push-button dispenser ensures quick and hassle-free access to the stored product whenever needed.

Notably, the Surf Band Pro gained widespread attention when it appeared on the popular TV show Shark Tank. During the episode, the company received a generous offer of $150,000 for 10% equity, demonstrating the product’s potential and market appeal. While the offer was declined, the exposure from Shark Tank helped catapult the Surf Band Pro into the spotlight.

“The Surf Band Pro is a game-changer for surfers and outdoor enthusiasts. It provides a practical and hands-free solution for carrying essential products, allowing users to focus on enjoying their favorite activities without any distractions.” – Mark Cuban, Shark Tank Investor

The Surf Band Pro has since gained a loyal customer base and positive reviews for its functionality, convenience, and unique design. With its versatile usage and durable construction, the Surf Band Pro has become a must-have accessory for those who enjoy spending time outdoors.
