The Innovative Design of HiccAway

The HiccAway device revolutionizes the way we tackle hiccups with its ingeniously designed L-shaped plastic straw. Dr. Ali Seifi, a renowned medical expert in Neurointensive Care, conceptualized and refined this groundbreaking product, combining his expertise in the field with innovative design principles.

The HiccAway design features a unique L-shape that sets it apart from traditional straws. With two small apertures at the base, this design disrupts the flow of liquids and activates nerves in the diaphragm and epiglottis, providing an effective remedy for hiccups. By leveraging suction and forced inspiratory swallow techniques, HiccAway offers a natural and efficient solution to hiccups.

Dr. Ali Seifi’s expertise in Neurointensive Care played a pivotal role in the development of HiccAway.
