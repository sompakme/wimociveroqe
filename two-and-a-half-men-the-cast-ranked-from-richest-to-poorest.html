'Two And A Half Men': The Cast Ranked From Richest To Poorest

Publish date: 2024-07-04

Two and a Half Men aired on CBS from September 22, 2003, to February 19, 2015. Chuck Lorre and Lee Aronsohn were responsible for creating the show. It followed the lives of two brothers and a young boy living together in a beachfront house in Malibu, California.

The show focused on the interactions and humorous situations that arose from the starkly different personalities of the main characters. Throughout its run, Two and a Half Men was known for its witty dialogue, situational comedy, and Charlie Sheen's character's womanizing antics.

In later seasons, after Charlie Sheen's departure due to personal issues, Ashton Kutcher joined the show as Walden Schmidt, a billionaire entrepreneur who buys the Harper house. The show continued to explore the dynamics between the characters with the addition of Walden.

Two and a Half Men received both critical and commercial success during its run, becoming one of the highest-rated sitcoms on television. However, it also garnered some controversy, particularly surrounding Charlie Sheen's departure and public comments about the show's creator and network. Nevertheless, the show remained a fan favorite and enjoyed a successful twelve-season run before concluding in 2015.

10 Ashton Kutcher

Net Worth: $200 Million

  • Ashton Kutcher as Walden Schmidt

Ashton Kutcher portrayed Walden Schmidt in the popular American television sitcom Two and a Half Men. He joined the show's cast in its ninth season, taking over as one of the main characters after Charlie Sheen's departure from the series. Walden Schmidt was a billionaire internet entrepreneur who bought the Malibu beach house previously owned by Charlie Harper (Charlie Sheen's character) after Charlie's supposed death.

Walden Schmidt was portrayed as a socially awkward and emotionally vulnerable character. Walden's storyline involved his desire to adopt a child, and this storyline explored his emotional journey and the challenges he faced as a single man seeking to adopt.

After buying the beach house, Walden becomes the new roommate of Alan Harper (played by Jon Cryer). The two characters developed a close friendship over the course of the series, with Alan becoming Walden's confidant and offering him advice on various aspects of life.

Kutcher made a great deal of money from his acting career. It was with his role on Two and a Half Men that Kutcher became one of the highest-paid actors on TV with a $750,000 per episode salary.

But it's with investments that Kutcher really increased his net worth.

Starting in 2010, Kutcher and his business partners invested a combined $3 million into Spotify, $2.5 into Airbnb, and $500,000 in Uber.

It was a result of investing in these companies that the group was able to start Sound Ventures LLC, a venture capital company. The company now has a portfolio worth $1 billion and is worth an estimated $4.5 billion.

Ashton Kutcher's net worth is $200 million.

9 Jon Cryer

Net Worth: $70 Million

  • Jon Cryer as Alan Harper

From 2003 to 2015, Jon Cryer skillfully embodied the character of Alan Harper in the popular television series Two and a Half Men. As the uptight and neurotic brother of Charlie Harper, Cryer's performance as the financially struggling chiropractor and loving father of Jake garnered immense praise from critics and viewers alike.

His exceptional portrayal earned him numerous award nominations and ultimately led to him receiving the prestigious Primetime Emmy Award for Outstanding Supporting Actor in a Comedy Series.

While Cryer has had an amazing acting career, it was his role on Two and a Half Men that really caused his net worth to spike. Cryer earned between $550,000 and $650,000 per episode and earning royalties from syndication, while Two and Half Men was still on, Cryer earned $100 million. He is poised to earn more as a result of his syndication deal.

Cryer's net worth is $70 million in 2024, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

8 Angus T. Jones

Net Worth: $20 Million

  • Angus T. Jones as Jake Harper

Angus T. Jones portrayed Jake Harper, the son of Alan Harper, in the long-running CBS sitcom. He joined the show's main cast at a young age and became one of the central characters.

Jones played the role of the adorable and naive Jake from the show's premiere in 2003 until he left the series during its tenth season in 2013. His character's interactions with his uncle, Charlie Harper (played by Charlie Sheen), and their caretaker, Berta (played by Conchata Ferrell), provided many comedic moments throughout the series.

Because of his time on Two and a Half Men, Jones became one of the highest-paid child actors in history. This was because at the height of the show, Jones was making $350,000 per episode.

Despite having retired from acting, Jones has managed to hold onto most of his earnings from the show and according to Celebrity Net Worth, has a $20 million fortune in 2024 to show for it.

Taylor is also known for standing by Charlie Sheen through his most difficult moments, and taking his side through the controversy that went on while filming.

3 Charlie Sheen

Net Worth: $3 Million

  • Charlie Sheen as Charlie Harper

Charlie Sheen, a very well known actor with many starring roles, portrayed the character Charlie Harper in Two and a Half Men. Charlie Harper was one of the main characters of the show, and his portrayal by Charlie Sheen became one of the defining aspects of the series. Charlie Harper was depicted as a womanizing, carefree, and hedonistic bachelor with a love for alcohol, gambling, and casual relationships. He lived a luxurious lifestyle in his beachfront house in Malibu, California, and made a living as a successful jingle writer.

The show's central premise revolved around the interactions between Charlie and his uptight and financially struggling brother, Alan Harper (played by Jon Cryer). After Alan's divorce, he moved in with Charlie, bringing along his young son, Jake (played by Angus T. Jones). The show explored the humorous situations that arose from their contrasting personalities and parenting styles. Throughout the series, Charlie's character had numerous short-term relationships with women, often emphasizing his womanizing tendencies.

With his time on Two and a Half Men, Sheen built up a net worth of $150 million between his salary of $1.25 million to $2 million per episode and royalties received from syndication.

Unfortunately, due to the amount he was paying his ex-wives for spousal support, which at one point amounted to $110,000 per month to two of the exes, in conjunction with $500,000 for child support, Sheen's fortune dwindled quickly. Sheen even sold some of his backend points from Two and a Half Men to square his debts but ended up spending a portion of it instead on drugs and companions.

While Sheen's child and spousal support have been decreased due to Sheen's income decreasing, he still has to pay monthly. As such, without a reliable income, Sheen would only continue to lose his fortune.

Charlie Sheen's net worth is $3 million in 2024, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

1 April Bowlby

April Bowlby portrayed Kandi, a beautiful but ditzy and naive young woman who becomes involved with Alan Harper. She landed the role directly after her first audition. Bowlby's performance as Kandi provided comic relief and contributed to the show's lighthearted and humorous tone.
