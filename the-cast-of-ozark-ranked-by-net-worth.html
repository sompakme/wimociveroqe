The Cast Of 'Ozark' Ranked By Net Worth

Publish date: 2024-09-12

The Byrdes are headed for absolute disaster. At least, that's certainly what it looks like ahead of the season 4 premiere on Netflix. Of course, there's likely to be quite a few tragedies to unfold in the super-sized final season of the show which will air in two parts. That's not to say that every character on Ozark is doomed. Although given the high-stakes, dark, and brutal world Bill Dubuque and Mark Williams have created, it certainly seems as though most of the characters will face the consequences of their actions. So many already have.

To be cast of Ozark is a gift. There's no doubt the numerous actors who have had their characters killed off in the first three seasons have benefited from the popularity of the show. This includes the likes of Jordana Spiro, Trevor Long, Tom Pelphrey, Jason Butler Harner, Janet McTeer, and Peter Mullan. All of whome have seen their net worths increase drastically. But what about the cast members on the show who live to see the beginning of season 4? Just how much money do they have going into the finale season?

11 Carson Holmes Is Worth $100,000

Carson Holmes, who plays Three on Ozark, is building a respectable net worth for himself, according to ArticleBio. This is especially true given the fact that his main source of income appears to come from Ozark. Although, he also played a small role in Amazon's Uncle Frank and in The Best of Enemies.

10 Skylar Gaertner Is Worth $300,000

Skylar is best-known for playing Jonah on Ozark but his role of Young Matt Murdock on Netflix's Daredevil and his work in acclaimed Independent films such as I Smile Back has also gained him some notoriety. Still, he's at the beginning of his career. According to StarsGab, Skylar is worth a healthy $300,000. And there's no doubt that number is growing rapidly.

9 Sofia Hublitz Is Worth $500,000

The young woman behind Charlotte Byrde is another star on the rise. While she's been cast in a couple of Louie CK projects, Sofia hasn't done much else other than Ozark. However, her supporting role on Ozark has earned her a cool half a million, according to The WikiFeed.

8 Kevin L. Johnson Is Worth $1.5 Million

Sam Dermody has been very lucky throughout the first three seasons of Ozark. Has this sealed his tragic fate in the upcoming fourth and final season? Perhaps. But in the meantime, the man who plays him has made quite a bit of money. Since 2009, Kevin L. Johnson has had small roles in numerous high-profile projects that have built his net worth and his reputation in the business.

7 Felix Solis Is Worth $1.5 Million

Felix Solis plays the big-bad in Ozark, Omar Navarro. While Idol Net Worth claims that the contents of his bank account and assets are worth around $5 million, most sites claim it's closer to $1.5 million. But that's pretty respectable. Aside from Ozark, Felix is known for his work in Made In Jersey, The Following, and The Good Wife.

6 Lisa Emery Is Worth $1.9 Million

Net Worth Post claims that Lisa Emery is worth just shy of $2 million. There's no doubt that playing the villainous Darlene Snell on Ozark accounts for most of it. However, Lisa has been working constantly in Hollywood for years. She's also known for her work in Jessica Jones, Louie, Madam Secretary, and various episodes of Law & Order.

5 Charlie Tahan Is Worth $2 Million

Playing Wyatt Langmore has made Charlie Tahan a star on the rise. At the time of this writing, Charlie is set to star in two new movies, one of which stars Christian Bale and Robert Duvall. Aside from Ozark, Charlie's high net worth is thanks to playing Scarewcrow on Gotham, and his roles in Wayward Pines, Frankenweenie, Law & Order: SVU, Blue Jasmine, and I Am Legend.

4 Damian Young Is Worth $3 Million

Damian Young is that actor that you recognize from everything but rarely remember his name. But you should, he's only one of the most remarkable character actors in the business. On Ozark, he plays the sneaky Jim Rattlesdorf. But he's also known for his work in Snowpiercer, The Trial Of The Chicago 7, Bordman, House of Cards, Californication, Damages, and As The World Turns.

3 Julia Garner Is Worth $3 Million

There's no doubt that Julia Garner is the biggest breakout star on Ozark. Playing Ruth Langmore has gifted her an Emmy and her very own upcoming series, Inventing Anna. Her growing net worth is definitely mostly due to her incredible work on Ozark but she has also appeared in The Americans, Maniac, Dirty John, Waco, and The Perks of Being A Wallflower.

2 Laura Linney Is Worth $10 Million

When it comes to respected thespians within the business, few get as much praise as Laura Linney does. While she may not be as famous in the mainstream as some of her contemporaries, there's no doubt that the three-time Academy Award nominee is a power player in the independent film business. She's just THAT good of an actor. So, it's no wonder she landed the co-starring role of Wendy Byrde on Ozark. She's also famous for her work in Love Actually, The Truman Show, The Big C, Mr. Holmes, John Adams, The Squid and the Whale and Frasier. But, according to Express.UK, it's Ozark that's contributing the most to her massive net worth.

1 Jason Bateman Is Worth $30 Million

Jason Bateman is marking around $300,000 per episode of Ozark heading into the final season. Of course, this is a nice addition is his enormous net worth. Jason is by far the most successful member of the Ozark cast. His career is nothing short of impressive. He first got his start on Little House On The Prairie in the early 1980s and has been working consistently since then. Among his most notable and profitable works are Zootopia, Juno, Hancock, Horrible Bosses, Couple's Retreat, and The Outsider. Then, of course, there's Arrested Development. According to People, Jason Bateman was making around $125,000 per episode of the acclaimed sitcom. Pair all this with his notable directing and producing career and you have one wealthy, wealthy man.
