Britney Spears Wants a 'Hot Nerd' With a 'Really Big Penis'

Publish date: 2024-07-28

Oh, baby, baby! Britney Spears knows exactly what she wants in a man. During the February 14 performance of her Piece of Me show in Las Vegas, the single pop star explained to the crowd that she’s looking for a geeky guy who’s well-endowed.

“I think it’s time to find a hot nerd, like, a really hot guy, with a really big penis,” Spears, 34, told concertgoers before picking out an audience member to join her on stage for a performance of her sexy 2007 track “Freakshow.”

This isn’t the first time the “Pretty Girls” songstress has sent a strong message to men during one of her Piece of Me concerts. In September, she bluntly stated to her audience, “Men, frankly, can suck my f—ing toe.”

After splitting from boyfriend of eight months Charlie Ebersol in June and briefly dating Lindsay Lohan’s ex Harry Morton last fall, the twice-wed Spears has been vocal about living the single life.

Just two days ago, the mom to Sean Preston, 10, and Jayden James, 9, poked fun at herself, Instagramming a meme that read, “My boyfriend is so Handsome, looking all invisible and s–t.”

Prior to her hilarious ‘gram, Spears posted a clip from Bridget Jones’ Diary in which Renee Zellweger lip-synchs along to Celine Dion’s “All by Myself,” joking that she “had to laugh because this is totally me from time to time!!! #Mood.”
