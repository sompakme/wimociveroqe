Kateřina Siniaková is a Czech professional tennis player who has won four major tournaments in women’s doubles, all with Barbora Krejková, including the 2018 French Open, 2018 Wimbledon Championships, 2021 French Open, and 2022 Australian Open.

Due to her success in the sports, many of her fans have been wondering how much she earns and what her net worth might be estimated to be.

Kateřina Siniaková Net Worth

Siniaková makes most of her money from playing tennis. It is reported that her current annual salary is around US$6,542,004 USD. According to multiple sources, her net worth is estimated to be between 1-2 million dollars.

Siniaková was born on 10 May 1996, in Hradec Králové, Czech Republic to a Czech mother Hana, an accountant, and Russian father Dmitry, a former boxer and her coach. Siniaková’s younger brother Daniel is also a professional tennis player.
