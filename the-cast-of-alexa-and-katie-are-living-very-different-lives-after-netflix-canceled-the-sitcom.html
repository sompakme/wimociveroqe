The Cast of Alexa And Katie Are Living Very Different Lives After Netflix Canceled The Sitcom

Publish date: 2024-09-13

Alexa & Katie was a sitcom on Netflix about family and friendship that ran for four seasons. However, the main emphasis was on Alexa and Katie’s friendship. While juggling the demands of high school, Alexa receives a cancer diagnosis.

Through it all, her best friend Katie sticks by her side. The show ended up being nominated for a Primetime Emmy for Outstanding Children’s Program in 2018. Unfortunately, in 2020 the series ended in cancelation. The actors went their separate ways after that. Here’s what the cast has been up to since the show ended.

8 What Is Paris Berelc Doing Now?

Paris Berelc starred as one of the most essential characters on Alexa & Katie. Berelc was unquestionably one of the emerging stars back in 2018. After Alexa & Katie was canceled, Berelc joined another Netflix sitcom series, The Crew. The actress who played a race car driver found that this was a more mature role for her. Berelc’s past roles were more kid/teen friendly.

According to Elite Daily, Berelc said, “it’s an adult sitcom, so that was different for me. I feel like I’ve been doing kids and family projects and The Crew was my first [show] where I got to be more mature". Unfortunately, the show got canceled after 1 season.

Her choice of more mature roles didn’t stop there. Berelc later starred in BuzzFeed’s comedy film 1UP (2022). According to BuzzFeed, she took on the role of Vivian, an accomplished esports player who chooses to create an all-female competitive squad. Berelec was later cast as a voice actor playing Black Widow/Monopoly Daughter in Robot Chicken (2021). Her most recent role was in Do Revenge (2022), a teen black comedy movie on Netflix.

7 What Is Isabel May Doing Now?

Since landing her first professional role as Katie in Netflix's Alexa & Katie, May is certainly making a name for herself in the acting world. According to her IMDb profile, In 2021, May starred in the highly-anticipated prequel “1883” to Taylor Sheridan's hit show Yellowstone. She took on the role of Elsa Dutton alongside Faith Hill and Tim McGraw. During the second season of CBS’s hit sitcom Young Sheldon, May took on the part of Veronica Duncan.

After appearing in Run Hide Fight, she signed on to the projects I Want You Back (an Amazon film) and The Moon & Back. May is set to star in an upcoming movie, The Smack. She has also been narrating the Paramount Plus series 1923, which is a follow-up to the series 1883, since 2022.

6 What Is Jolie Jenkins Doing Now?

Jolie Jenkins cherished the time she spent on Alexa & Katie. She has since been cast in a few TV roles and a film. She had a guest role in Disney Channel's Sydney to the Max (2021) as Gemma. In the Nickelodeon comedy series, The Loud House, Jenkins currently stars as Rita Loud. She also had a leading role in the religious drama Jesus Revolution (2023).

According to Bump Club Beyond, Jenkins mentioned that playing Jennifer Cooper on Alexa & Katie was her “favorite role to date.” It’s the longest job I have ever had. What I love about JC and what I love about playing her is that she’s so comfortable at not being perfect,” she continued.

The actress is happily married to designer and artist David Pagani, with whom she has two sons. Jenkins frequently shares images of her family and herself on Instagram.

5 What Is Emery Kelly Doing Now?

Kelly is both an actor and singer. After Alexa & Katie, he co-starred in season one of the Disney+ drama Big Shot, alongside John Stamos, according to American Songwriter. He is currently focused on his career as a musician and released his single SOME Life It Is in 2022.

Kelly discussed the tracking saying, "Life gives us the opportunity to put our life into ‘something’ and when you achieve your ‘something’ through all the waves of your success and failures you can look back and say “SOME Life It Is”. He is active on both Tik Tok and Instagram, posting frequent content about his music and lifestyle.

4 What Is Eddie Shin Doing Now?

Before having a starring role in Alexa & Katie, Shin had already established himself in the industry with his lengthy acting career. According to The List, Alex & Katie help demonstrate his acting ability. Shin doesn't appear to have recently secured any roles, indicating that he may have taken a break from acting.

Shin leads a rather typical lifestyle. He doesn't share anything about his private life, and there isn't much news about him online. Shin rarely uploads photos, and when he does, they are mainly of himself at work. Shin also has a Twitter account, and most of its tweets are shared from his Instagram.

3 What Is Finn Carr Doing Now?

Finn Carr, now 14 years old, has quite an intriguing acting background. Carr was just ten years old when Alexa & Katie aired. After the show ended, he went on to star in popular shows like iCarly (2021 reboot).

He was also featured in three episodes of General Hospital (2022) as Rocco Falconeri. Carr is currently slated to star in a Disney Channel series, Pretty Freekin Scary, which is in production.

2 What Is Jack Griffo Doing Now?

Jack Griffo has the best of both worlds; he acts and sings. Griffo has a YouTube channel where he performs music cover versions. According to National Today, once the show ended, he continued landing television roles and starred in The 2nd as Sean Davis, Butter as Parker, and The Christmas High Note as Todd.

Griffo also landed a voice role where he portrayed Damian Wayne in the film Batman and Superman: Battle of the Super Sons. As for his love life, he dated his co-star Paris Berelc for a while, but they broke up, according to J-14.

1 What Is Tiffani Thiessen Doing Now?

Thiessen starred in the Saved by the Bell revival that ran on Peacock from 2020 to 2021 after the Alexa & Katie ended. Despite only having 20 episodes, the show ran for two seasons. Thiessen currently serves as both host and producer on MTV's Deliciousness.

According to people, Thiessen will catch up with her Beverly Hills 90210 co-stars at the second annual 90s Con held at Connecticut Convention Center this coming March. She is a very active user on Instagram, posting several pictures of her family, food, and herself to her 1.8 million Instagram followers.
