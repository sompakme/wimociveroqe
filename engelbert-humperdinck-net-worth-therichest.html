Engelbert Humperdinck has an estimated net worth of $90 million. Busy as ever with his touring schedule,Humperdinck has sold 150 million records over his career, resulting in 64 gold and 24 platinum albums. He plays 50-75 shows a year, predominately in casinos and theatres in North America.

There are 40 shows planned for 2011. Humperdinck went into tax exile in America in the early 1970s. In his autobiography, he makes a brief reference to his wealth: “All I am prepared to say is that, during my first US tour of 1971, I did make, and continued to make thereafter, an awful lot of money.” He adds later that along the way he also lost a fortune to various “thieves”.
