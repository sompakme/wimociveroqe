Kevin Owens Reflects On Having Back-To-Back WrestleMania Main Events

Publish date: 2024-08-16

KO-Mania has been taken to the next level with Kevin Owens main-eventing WrestleMania two years in a row.

RAW Superstar Kevin Owens was a recent guest on Love Wrestling to discuss a wide variety of subjects. When asked if one WrestleMania event meant more to him than the other, after some reflection, KO admitted that main eventing WrestleMania 38 with Stone Cold Steve Austin just edges out this year by a little bit.

“I hate doing this because it sounds like I’m trying to get out of it easily, but there’s really no words to describe either experience. The best way I can describe it is I look back on both of these nights, and I kind of can’t believe they happened that I found myself in that situation in that spot getting to do that. And the circumstances in both years were just so incredible.

“But I guess if one takes the absolute cake and you know it just edges out the other one by very little. I think it’s still more unbelievable to me that I wrestled Stone Cold Steve Austin 19 years after his last match in the main event of WrestleMania.

“Like that’s just — I don’t know. It’s just kind of a pinch yourself to make sure it’s a real kind of moment. And I’ve done that a lot over the last year and a half or so since that happened. But both those moments are just things I’ll look back on when I’m done and think I was very, very lucky to get to experience those things.”
