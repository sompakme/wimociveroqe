Kimberly Stewart News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-07-07

Name:Kimberly Stewart

Hometown:Los Angeles, CA

Birthday:August 21, 1979

About

Along with Paris Hilton, Tara Reid, and Lindsay Lohan, Kimberly Stewart is one of Hollywood’s most notorious party girls. Stewart is the daughter of Rod Stewart and Alana Stewart.

Aside from being the legendary rocker’s daughter, Stewart is most famous for her work as a model. In 2005, she signed on to be an underwear model with the Australian company, Antz Pantz.

For a brief time, Stewart was engaged to Laguna Beach star Talan Torriero in 2005. Six years later, Benicio del Toro‘s rep confirmed Stewart was pregnant with his child. Stewart gave birth to a baby girl in August 2011.
