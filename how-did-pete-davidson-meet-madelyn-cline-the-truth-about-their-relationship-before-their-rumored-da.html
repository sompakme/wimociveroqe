Madelyn Cline's love life has been a topic of interest ever since her breakout role in Netflix's Outer Banks in 2020. While fans were shipping her and her co-star Chase Stokes on screen, their real-life romance was no secret. Following their breakup, Cline was romantically linked to several other celebrities, including Ross Butler and Zack Bia. She also had a relationship with musician Jackson Guthy.

However, things got intriguing when Cline posted a photo on Instagram showing her with a mysterious man at a New York Jets NFL game in New Jersey. Fans quickly speculated that this mystery man could be none other than SNL alum Pete Davidson.

On the other hand, Davidson has been involved in a series of high-profile romances with A-list celebrities, including model Emily Ratajkowski, Kim Kardashian, Phoebe Dynevor, and Ariana Grande.

How Did Pete Davidson And Madelyn Cline Meet?

Us Weekly was the first to report on the budding romance between Madelyn Cline and Pete Davidson. According to the publication, Davidson and Cline found love with each other after parting ways with their previous partners.

Pete Davidson ended his relationship with Chase Sui Wonders, a romance that had lasted less than a year. An insider informed Entertainment Tonight on August 2023 that "Pete Davidson and Chase Sui Wonders have broken up and both of them are focusing on themselves."

Other reports hinted that their breakup was due to things naturally coming to an end. "There's no bad blood or any hard feelings between them. Their relationship had simply run its course, and they decided they just wanted different things," the insider told Us Weekly.

In September 2023, Us Weekly confirmed the relationship between Davidson and Cline. According to a source, "Madelyn and Pete had hoped to remain low-key for a while, especially because Madelyn is a really private person." However, their bond grew remarkably strong in a short period, leading to them spending significant time together.

Fans were taken aback by how swiftly their connection developed. Although the exact event of how their new romance started remains uncertain, it seems they attempted to keep their relationship discreet due to the attention garnered by their recent high-profile breakups.

What Did Pete Davidson And Madelyn Cline Say About Their Relationships?

The public has maintained a persistent interest in Pete Davidson's love life, though he's expressed that it's not a major concern for him. During the season 2 premiere of Peacock's Hart to Heart in 2022, he shared, "I don't mind. I know what it comes with. I totally get it because whatever I get to do is sick. If that's all you have to deal with then whatever."

Davidson, who has become known for his relationships with high-profile women, opened up about the public fascination with his romantic life and how it impacted his time on SNL. He stated that the eight seasons he spent on the late-night sketch comedy show were somewhat marred by the public's curiosity about his dating life.

"I'm not, like, flexing, you know what I mean? And these people that I've dated, I met them at work," he remarked about his famous partners. "I wasn't in anyone's DMs, no one was in mine. ... Suddenly you're in this zeitgeist and that has nothing to do with the work. And that's a really s—-y feeling." he shared on the Real Ones podcast in March 2023.

While Madelyn Cline ended her relationship with Chase Stokes in November 2021, the actress has since expressed her desire to keep her personal life out of the public eye. In February 2023, she told Today, "Some people can discuss their personal life—I can't. If you give everything, like, 100 percent of your energy, away, where's the rest for yourself? You finally understand why people in the public eye keep their circles small."

She added, "I realized there are parts of my life that only me or one other person are entitled to. I prefer it that way. It also just makes things more special. That's usually how relationships are."
