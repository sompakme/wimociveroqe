Indian businessman Benu Gopal Bangur has an estimated net worth of $6.48 billion as of July 2017, according to Bloomberg. Currently, he works for Shree Cement Ltd as the non-executive chairman. Until January 2012, he served as the company's executive chairman.

Benu Gopal Bangur currently controls Shree Cement, a cement making company based in North Indian. Since 2002, his son has been running the company which is considered as one of the best in its field.

He also sits as the Chairman of the Board of NBI Industrial Finance Co Ltd. and since October 1979, he has been working as the Director of Shree Cement Ltd. He also serves as the Director of Khemka Properties Pvt. Ltd., Digvijay Finlease Ltd., The Didwana Industrial Corporation Ltd., and NBI Industrial Finance Co. Ltd.
