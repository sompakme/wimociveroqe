Fans Noticed Just How Taylor Swift Flirts Relentlessly With Reporters To Win Them Over

Publish date: 2024-08-28

Highlights

  • Taylor Swift flirts with reporters regularly during interviews.
  • Some fans speculate that Swift flirts on purpose to make reporters like her more.

If a reporter is lucky enough to chat with Taylor Swift, they're going to ask about her incredible music... and maybe hope that she will share a tidbit about her love life. Of course, if someone is a fan, they know that the singer would rather not be asked any questions about her rich and famous ex-boyfriends.

While Taylor Swift's 2023 Time Person Of The Year interview is one of her best since she opened up so much, there are many examples of unforgettable, engaging interviews. After watching the beloved singer chat on red carpets and in video interviews, fans realized something compelling: Taylor Swift tends to flirt with reporters regularly.

Fans Realized Taylor Swift Flirts With Reporters During Many Interviews

Taylor Swift flirts during so many interviews that a fan put together a compilation video. When appearing on The Graham Norton Show, the singer complimented Saoirse Ronan for starring in Hanna, the 2011 action movie. She said, "I literally wanted to fight after I saw you in that movie."

Ronan looked touched and said that she would teach her. Swift said "yes" and added, "So good. And you carried that whole movie, it was like just you in so much of the movie." Norton joked that it was "a lovefest" between the two of them.

When she was asked if she had a date, Swift said "I'm dateless... I never have a date to these things, you know that."

At the end of 2023, fans couldn't stop talking about the time when Ellen DeGeneres interviewed Taylor Swift in 2013 and wouldn't stop asking about her "boyfriend" Zac Efron.
