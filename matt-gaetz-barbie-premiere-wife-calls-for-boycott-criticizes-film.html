Matt Gaetz Barbie Premiere: Wife Calls For Boycott &amp; Criticizes Film

Publish date: 2024-08-30

The Matt Gaetz Barbie Premiere was a star-studded event filled with glamour and anticipation as guests gathered to witness the launch of the much-anticipated Barbie movie adaptation.

Rep. Matt Gaetz’s wife, Ginger Gaetz, attended the Barbie movie’s pink carpet premiere at the British embassy in Washington, D.C. 

Ginger Gaetz expressed disappointment with the movie’s values and criticized Ryan Gosling’s portrayal of Ken, calling it “low T” or low testosterone.

In a tweet, Ginger Gaetz shared her disappointment with the 2023 Barbie movie, stating that the Barbie she grew up with represented limitless possibilities, diverse careers, and feminine empowerment. 

Ginger Gaetz voiced her concerns about the movie, pointing out the unfortunate portrayal of big dreams causing anxiety instead of inspiration, the low testosterone depiction of Ken, and the unfair treatment of pregnant Barbie Midge. 

She also criticized Ryan Gosling’s performance, describing it as having “beta energy.”

However, she felt that the new movie neglected to address notions of faith and family, and she disapproved of the portrayal of men and women unable to collaborate positively.

Early Viewing and Director Q&A Session

Matt Gaetz Barbie movie’s early viewing at the British embassy provided attendees with a chance to hear from director Greta Gerwig during a Q&A session.

The Barbie movie, starring Margot Robbie and Ryan Gosling, was made in the U.K. and showcased at the British embassy. 

Attendees, including members of the Washington Spirit women’s basketball team and politicians, had the opportunity to watch the movie early and participate in a Q&A session with director Greta Gerwig.

UK-US Film Industry Collaboration

The movie’s production in the UK highlights the collaborative relationship between the UK and the U.S. film industry. 

Warner Brothers’ significant presence in the UK made it an ideal filming location for the Barbie movie, strengthening the UK-US relationship in the film sector.

The British ambassador’s residence was transformed into Barbie’s dream home for the premiere, exuding an English country house atmosphere. 

Guests enjoyed pink-themed decorations, including disco balls hanging from rose bushes, and indulged in pink burgers and sushi.

Republicans’ outrage and controversy in the Movie

Republicans expressed anger over a crayon map in the film featuring the “nine-dash line,” which represents Chinese authorities’ claim over the South China Sea. 

Some politicians accused Hollywood of endorsing Chinese Communist Party propaganda.

Praise for Margot Robbie and the movie’s unique approach

Despite Ginger Gaetz’s initial criticisms of the Barbie movie, she couldn’t help but appreciate the performance delivered by Margot Robbie. 

She flawlessly portrayed the iconic doll on the big screen. 

Ginger’s reservations about the movie’s values and portrayal of Ken were tempered by her admiration for the intricate and imaginative costume design that brought the characters to life in a visually stunning manner. 

The accompanying soundtrack, carefully curated to enhance the film’s charm, received praise from Ginger and movie critics who commended its unique and refreshing approach.

In Summary

Ginger Gaetz’s attendance at the Barbie movie premiere sparked a call for a boycott and highlighted her concerns about the film’s values. The Barbie movie showcased a collaboration between the UK and US film industries, with the British embassy serving as Barbie’s dream home for the event. 

While the movie received praise for its unique approach, it also faced controversies over its depiction of Chinese territorial claims.
